What’s AltControl and Track 4?

W6P EU has three tracks, but we’re here to introduce Track 4, the alternative space for ideas that might not fit the official program. AltControl is a playful nod to the talks that won’t be heard on the main stage, but are essential to rethinking the future of the W7S project.

In this informal setting (yes, a bar!), we’re bringing together speakers for quick, impactful talks on topics like governance, freedom of expression, decentralization, and other issues that challenge the current direction of the web. Drinks in hand we will have raw talks about alternative paths for a W7S and beyond.

Secret Location, Exciting Vibes

To keep the atmosphere laid-back and free from unintended changes, the venue will be kept top secret until the last moment. You’ll get the details by email just before the event. Anonymity for attendees is a priority—your identity will remain private by default unless you choose to engage more publicly during the event.

Free Entry, Big Impact

AltControl is free to attend, but donations are welcome. Any remaining sponsorship or entey funds will be evenly distributed to our speakers, ensuring their contributions are supported. At the event, participants will also vote on what to do with the AltControl brand, domains, logo, and IP we want to give it away to a foundarion with good governace, and you will decide on the night!
