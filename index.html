Our Inaugural Alt Ctrl Org Track 4 event will be in Basel 2025

Join us on Friday Evening (after wordcamp), June 6th, in Basel to hear alternative talks on wordpress that you wont hear at wordcamp. Attend your usual conference then come on down, it's not an either or, you can be affiliated with anyone in the community and attend both events!

What is Alt Ctrl Org and Track 4

We offer an alternative track, Track 4, the evening track or meet up that is an alternative space for ideas that might not fit the official program. Alt Ctrl Org is the physical space where people can meet, talk and think outside the box.

In this informal setting (yes, a bar!), we’re bringing together speakers for quick, high impact talks on topics like governance, freedom of expression, decentralization, and other challenging issues. Drinks in hand we will have talks about organising alternative paths for a alternative unified future.

Independent Governance

Alt Ctrl Org will give voices to different, novel and challenging ideas, it will be free to attend, but small donations are welcome. Any remaining sponsorship or entry donations will be evenly distributed to our speakers and volunteers on the night, ensuring their contributions are supported.

If you’re interested in joiningg our board email us and let us know altctrlorg@proton.me. We need you!
