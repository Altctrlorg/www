AltControl: Track 4 – The W6P EU sideloading Party

Join us on Saturday, June 7th, in Basel for a bold, addition to the W6P EU experience. Welcome to AltControl: Track 4 – the track that wasn’t invited, but might just offer a new perspective on where things are heading.

What’s AltControl: Track 4?

W6P EU has three tracks—but we’re here to introduce Track 4, the alternative space for ideas that might not fit the official program. AltControl is a playful nod to the talks that won’t be heard on the main stage, but are essential to rethinking the future of the W7S project.

In this informal setting (yes, a bar!), we’re bringing together speakers for quick, impactful talks on topics like governance, freedom of expression, decentralization, and other issues that challenge the current direction of the web. Drinks in hand, raw talks about alternative paths for W7S and beyond.

Secret Location, Exclusive Vibes

To keep the atmosphere laid-back and free from interference, the venue will be kept secret until the last moment. You’ll get the details by email just before the event. Anonymity for attendees is a priority—your identity will remain private by default unless you choose to engage more publicly during the event.

Free Entry, Big Impact

AltControl is free to attend, but donations are welcome. Any remaining sponsorship funds will be evenly distributed to our speakers, ensuring their contributions are supported. At the event, participants will also vote on which foundation or cause will receive the AltControl brand, logo, and IP—helping the legacy of this event support something meaningful.

Call for Speakers

Do you have a bold idea, a provocative topic, or a unique perspective that challenges the current direction of W7S, or explores issues like web governance, freedom of expression, or decentralization? We’re inviting passionate speakers to give short, punchy talks in a relaxed, intimate setting.

No slides. No formalities. Just you and your ideas.

If you’re interested in speaking at AltControl: Track 4, submit your talk proposal to altctrl@proton.me. We welcome talks that challenge the status quo and spark conversations that may not happen in the traditional tracks.
