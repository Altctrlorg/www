What is AltControl and Track 4

WordCamp EU has three tracks, but we’re here to introduce Track 4, the alternative space for ideas that might not fit the official program. AltControl is the physicl space where people can meet, talk and think outside the box.

In this informal setting (yes, a bar!), we’re bringing together speakers for quick, impactful talks on topics like governance, freedom of expression, decentralization, and other challenging issues. Drinks in hand we will have raw talks about alternative paths for a future.

Secret Location, Exciting Vibes

To keep the atmosphere laid-back and free from unwanted changes, the venue will be announced much neared the date. You’ll get the details by email just before the event. Anonymity for attendees is a priority your identity will not be published and will remain private by default unless you choose to engage more publicly during the event.

Free Entry, Big Impact

AltControl is free to attend, but small donations are welcome. Any remaining sponsorship or entey funds will be evenly distributed to our speakers on the night, ensuring their contributions are supported. At the event, participants will also vote on what to do with the AltControl brand, domains, logo, and IP and you will decide on the night with one vote per atteendee!

Call for Speakers

Do you have a bold idea, a provocative topic, or a unique perspective that challenges the status quo, or explores issues like governance, freedom of expression, deplatforming or decentralization? We’re inviting passionate speakers to give short, punchy talks in a relaxed, intimate setting.

No formalities. Just you, your ideas, a laptop and a mic.

If you’re interested in speaking at AltControl: Track 4, submit your talk proposal to altctrlorg@proton.me. We welcome talks that challenge the status quo and spark conversations that may not happen in the traditional tracks.
