Volunteers Wanted

Want to be part of the team that makes Alt Ctrl Org happen? We’re looking for volunteers to help with planning, design, dec, logistics, attendee check-ins, event setup, and more.

By volunteering, you’ll get a behind-the-scenes look at the event, meet amazing people, and play a vital role in creating an unforgettable experience.

We are looking for experienced pros right the way to rookies, if you have lead an event before your welcome to teach us a thinkg or two.

What we need

  • Event organisers and leads
  • Runners
  • PR
  • Social
  • Design
  • Developers
  • Translators
  • MCs
  • Swag station

If you’re interested in volunteering contact us at altctrlorg@proton.me and let us know how you want to get involved. Remain anonymous if you wish.
